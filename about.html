About Krishna

Krishna is revered as the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the embodiment of compassion, wisdom, and divine play. His stories illuminate dharma, devotion, and the path of love toward the divine.

Life of Krishna

Born in Mathura, Krishna grew up in Vrindavan and Dvārakā. His childhood leelas (divine plays) teach courage, humility, and the joy of divine love. His flute calls the soul to remember the eternal divine.

Teachings & Devotion

Krishna’s teachings emphasize bhakti (devotion) over ritualism, the importance of dharma (righteousness), and the unity of the self with the divine. The Bhagavad Gita remains a guiding compass for seekers.
